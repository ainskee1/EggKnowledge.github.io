What is Egg Knowledge?

Egg Knowledge is a website dedicated to educating the American consumers about the egg industry. We want to make sure that the everyday egg consumer is aware of the practices of the egg industry, ranging from the health and enviroment the birds are kept in, to the marketing and cost of the eggs sold.
